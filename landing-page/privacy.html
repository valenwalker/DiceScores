Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

We do not require you to create an account. All game data (player names, scores, match history) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Analytics (Firebase)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data, including:

  • App opens, session duration, and feature interactions
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • In-app purchase events (anonymous)

This data helps us understand how the app is used and improve the experience. It does not identify you personally.

Advertising (Google Mobile Ads)

The free version of the app displays ads served by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use data to show you personalized ads, including:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Coarse location (country/region)
  • Device information

You can opt out of personalized ads at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising. Purchasing a premium plan removes all ads from the app.

2. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Transaction records are managed by Apple under their own Privacy Policy.

3. Data Storage

All game data (scores, player names, match history, settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs. This data:

  • Is not uploaded to any server
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • May be backed up via iCloud if you have iCloud Backup enabled

5. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Anonymized analytics data is retained by Firebase according to Google's data retention policies. Local app data remains on your device until you delete the app.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access the data held about you, request deletion of your data, or opt out of analytics and personalized advertising.

To opt out of personalized ads, go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalized Ads (off).

To request data deletion or for any privacy-related questions, contact us directly at the address below.
